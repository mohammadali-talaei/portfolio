About ProTech:

History and Vision of Company

ProTech is a weed company with a goal to help medium to large enterprises collaborate on the design, documentation, and implementation of their business processes. ProTech has been in business since 1998 and we try to provide a friendly community for our employees to know ProTech as their second home.

- Technology Environment:
The ProTech application and the testing, staging, and DR environments are hosted in Microsoft Azure. Familiarity with cloud hosting services would be beneficial. We adhere to a DevOps development methodology utilizing Microsoft DevOps for managing the code and work items.

- Office Environment:
We support a hybrid work environment, meaning employees can work 50% from home and 50% in the office. Our offices are located just steps from the High Park Subway in the heart of Toronto's vibrant Bloor West neighborhood. A large selection of restaurants, shopping, and fitness facilities are all within a short walk of the office.

- Inclusive Environment:
At ProTech, we are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where all employees feel valued, respected, and supported. We are dedicated to building a workforce that reflects the diversity of our great city.



Job Description

We are seeking a Software Developer to become an integral part of our team! You will be responsible for creating, modifying, and supporting our proprietary ProTech software.

- Why Work Here?
“At ProTech, we are very proud of our close-knit team and the willingness of the employees to support and assist each other.”
The successful candidate must be eligible to work in Canada. ProTech does not offer relocation or immigration assistance.

- Your job:
As a Developer, you will be responsible for developing and enhancing the platform to meet the needs of the business. You have a solution-focused mindset and the ability to provide excellent customer service, training, and support. Candidates must be passionate about technology, love customer service, and have the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment. Some travel may be required.

Responsibilities and Duties

Responsibility and Duties
- Collaborate with key business partners and stakeholders to build out a mature asset lifecycle management function with processes and workflows that align with industry standards and best practices. - Design technical solutions and implementations of strategic technologies
- Participate in and contribute to client meetings and proposals
- Participate in technical design and planning discussions. - Work closely with our designers on implementing designs into code.
- Work closely with our product managers to ensure projects are delivered in a timely fashion. - Understanding the business need and work in the same direction as the company wants in order to achieve the goal of company.
- Hands-on troubleshooting, debugging and fixing of issues during development and post-release. - Work closely with stakeholders to develop clear, concise, and accurate documentation such as user requirements, workflow and use case diagrams, user acceptance criteria.
- Contribute to internal and end-user documentation that relates to your work - Lead, mentor and coach our developers to level them up in their craft
- Support the development and growth of the services delivered by the organization. -Being respectful to everyone.
